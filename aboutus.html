About Us



Food blog is the collaborative outcome of seasoned food & business operators who
have combined their past years industry experience to create this exciting dining offer.
We believe in creating interesting and unique places to enjoy our simple and great tasting
“Real Italian” food. Sometimes we pop up in shopping centres, sometimes in a great shopping strip.


Either way we’ll make sure you feel like you’re in our home and ready for some of our great, simple food.
At Easy, we're constantly pushing ourselves to find new ways to redefine the dining experience and we are wholeheartedly embracing small shareable plates and large shareable meats, with house-made pasta and all the oysters and raw bar ingredients we can find.
